# Project Charter

## Project name
**Aarohan CareerOS**

## Six-month outcome
- Land a stable, AI-enhanced technical leadership role.
- Target base salary: $200,000+ plus benefits.
- Secondary acceptable band: base $180,000–$199,999 only when total compensation exceeds $200,000 and the role offers strong stability, remote flexibility, and leadership growth.
- Build a consulting pipeline around AI-assisted Quality Engineering transformation.

## Primary positioning
The candidate is not a generic AI generalist.

The candidate is a senior technical leader with:
- a software engineering foundation;
- enterprise application development experience;
- Quality Engineering and SDET leadership;
- automation, API, performance, CI/CD, and test-platform experience;
- offshore-team leadership;
- delivery ownership;
- growing AI workflow, agentic-system, prompt, and context-engineering capability.

## Core product outcomes
- discover fresh, relevant roles;
- score role fit and risk;
- tailor truthful resumes;
- prepare outreach;
- track applications;
- detect interview signals;
- create a role-specific Interview Grilling Machine;
- track consulting opportunities;
- provide analytics and next actions.

## Human approval boundary
The system must stop before:
- final job application submission;
- external message sending;
- legal or demographic attestation;
- salary commitment;
- work-authorization or immigration answers;
- background-check answers;
- any unsupported professional claim.
