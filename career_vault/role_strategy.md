# Role Strategy

## Recommendation
Primary campaign: Quality Engineering leadership and architecture.

This is the best risk-adjusted route to $200,000+ because it combines:
- 15+ years of engineering experience;
- development credibility;
- enterprise delivery;
- current Quality Engineering leadership;
- automation, API, performance, CI/CD, and governance;
- AI-assisted engineering differentiation.

9. GenAI / LLM Quality Engineering Lead

11. Solution Architect — Quality, DevOps, and AI Enablement
12. Performance Engineering / Reliability Test Lead

## Secondary role families
- Engineering Manager for developer productivity or quality platforms
- Technical Program Lead for AI-assisted engineering transformation
- Senior Solutions Architect with strong quality and delivery ownership
- Fractional Quality Engineering leader
- AI workflow automation consultant

## Avoid as primary campaign
- generic AI generalist;
- pure prompt engineer without domain depth;
- pure software architect roles requiring current large-scale architecture ownership that cannot be demonstrated;
- junior or mid-level SDET roles;
- roles capped materially below target compensation;
- unstable startups with unclear runway and no upside.
