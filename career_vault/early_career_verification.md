# Early Career Verification Checklist

Do not publish the early consulting history until verified.

Collect:
- original resumes;
- offer letters;
- W-2 or 1099 employer names;
- LinkedIn history;
- old email records;
- university OPT/CPT records if relevant;
- exact month/year for each assignment;
- defensible job titles;
- project summaries that do not rely on fabricated history.

The system must not repeat or preserve any knowingly false resume claim.
Use verified skills and actual work performed.
