# Product Scope

## Career operations
- job-source ingestion;
- job normalization;
- deduplication;
- freshness classification;
- salary and benefits analysis;
- remote/location analysis;
- role-family matching;
- stability and risk analysis;
- evidence-based fit scoring;
- tailored resume generation;
- cover letters and outreach drafts;
- application approval queue;
- application tracking;
- recruiter-signal detection;
- interview preparation;
- conversion analytics.

## Interview Grilling Machine
- company briefing;
- role decomposition;
- resume-to-JD challenge map;
- technical questions;
- architecture questions;
- leadership questions;
- behavioral questions;
- AI/agentic-system questions;
- recommended answer frameworks;
- grounded sample answers;
- exercises;
- take-home simulations;
- progress tracking;
- voice mock-interview handoff.

## Consulting track
- lead intake;
- company/problem qualification;
- service recommendation;
- proposal draft;
- case-study matching;
- follow-up tracker;
- pipeline analytics.

## Not in V1
- automatic final submission;
- LinkedIn scraping;
- Indeed scraping;
- autonomous external messaging;
- full social-media publishing;
- video rendering;
- bookkeeping;
- LLC formation.
