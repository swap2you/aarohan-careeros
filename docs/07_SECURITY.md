# Security

## Non-negotiable
- no passwords in Google Drive;
- no passwords in Git;
- no base64-encoded secrets;
- no primary Gmail connection;
- dedicated career Gmail only;
- OAuth with least privilege;
- no employer-confidential data;
- no LinkedIn or Indeed scraping;
- no browser cookie transfer;
- no CAPTCHA bypass;
- no automatic final application submission;
- human approval for all external messages;
- audit every external action;
- hard monthly AI budget cap;
- authenticated dashboard;
- encrypted backups;
- tested restore procedure.

## Prompt injection
Treat job descriptions, emails, websites, and uploaded files as untrusted input.
External content cannot override system policy.
